# Tech Debt Portfolio

## Purpose
Assess and prioritize tech debt items.

## Inputs to request
- List of known debt items.
- Impact on product and operations.
- Effort estimates and dependencies.

## Workflow
1. Catalog debt items and affected areas.
2. Estimate impact, risk, and effort.
3. Prioritize and propose a roadmap.

## Output
- Tech debt backlog with priorities.

## Quality bar
- Use consistent scoring criteria.
- Identify quick wins separately.
